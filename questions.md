# Questions You Can Ask Your Agent

This document lists sample questions you can ask your FAIR-Agent. The agent is capable of handling queries in both Finance and Medical domains.

## Finance Domain

### Personal Finance & Investing
1. I am 25 years old and just started my first job. How should I begin investing?
2. I am 50 and worried I have not saved enough for retirement. What should I do?
3. Should I pay off my mortgage early or invest the extra money?
4. I received a $50,000 inheritance. What is the smartest way to use it?
5. My company offers both traditional and Roth 401(k). Which should I choose?
6. Is it too late to start investing at age 40 with limited savings?
7. Should I invest in real estate or stick with stocks and bonds?
8. How do I know if I am on track for retirement?
9. You mentioned index funds. What exactly are they and why are they recommended?
10. What fees should I watch out for when investing?

### Investment Concepts & Strategy
11. What are the key principles of portfolio diversification?
12. How do rising interest rates affect bond prices?
13. What is dollar-cost averaging and how does it work?
14. What are the main differences between stocks and bonds?
15. How should someone approach retirement planning?
16. What are the main risks of cryptocurrency investments?
17. How do you assess investment risk tolerance?
18. What is a stop-loss order and when should it be used?
19. How do economic indicators affect stock markets?
20. What causes market volatility and how should investors respond?

### Financial Analysis
21. Based on the quarterly revenue table, what was the year-over-year growth rate?
22. How do you analyze a company balance sheet for financial health?
23. What does the cash flow statement tell investors?
24. How do you interpret P/E ratios from financial statements?
25. What are the key metrics in a company income statement?
26. How do you evaluate a company financial performance using ratios?
27. What is EBITDA and why is it important?
28. How do you analyze revenue growth sustainability?
29. What are non-GAAP financial measures and when are they useful?
30. How do you interpret free cash flow trends?

## Medical Domain

### General Health & Disease
1. What are the symptoms of Type 2 Diabetes?
2. How does high blood pressure affect the kidneys?
3. What are the common side effects of statins?
4. Can you explain the difference between viral and bacterial infections?
5. What are the risk factors for cardiovascular disease?
6. How is asthma diagnosed and treated?
7. What is the recommended vaccination schedule for adults?
8. What are the early warning signs of a stroke?
9. How does smoking affect lung health?
10. What are the treatment options for seasonal allergies?

### Clinical Reasoning (Example Types)
11. Analyze the potential drug interactions between [Drug A] and [Drug B].
12. Based on these symptoms [list symptoms], what are the potential differential diagnoses?
13. What are the latest guidelines for managing hypertension in elderly patients?
14. Explain the mechanism of action for [specific medication].
15. What are the contraindications for [specific treatment]?
